# Competition Results - Quick Guide

## For Range Officers

### Entering Results During Competition

1. Navigate to your competition page
2. Click on the **"Results"** tab
3. The **"Registrera Resultat"** section shows the entry keypad
4. Select team, shooter, and series using the navigation buttons
5. Enter shots using keyboard (0-9, X) or click the buttons
6. Press **ENTER** to save
7. System automatically moves to next shooter/series based on your settings

**Important**: Multiple range officers can work at the same time on different shooters!

### Creating the Results List

1. While in the **"Registrera Resultat"** section
2. Scroll to the bottom-right of the keypad
3. Click **"Skapa Resultatlista"**
4. Wait a few seconds - success message will appear
5. The **"Resultatlista"** section will appear with all results

**Tip**: Click this button anytime during the competition to update the results list with new entries.

## For Competition Administrators

### Viewing Live Results

1. Navigate to Competition → **Resultat Hub** → **Slutresultat**
2. While preliminary:
   - Shows **yellow "PRELIMINÄR"** badge
   - Auto-refreshes every 15 seconds
   - Can pause/resume auto-refresh if needed

### Making Results Official

1. Go to Competition → **Results** tab
2. Expand the **"Resultatlista"** section
3. Review all results carefully
4. Click **"Markera som Officiell"** (green button)
5. Confirm the action
6. Results are now official with **green "OFFICIELL"** badge
7. Auto-refresh stops automatically

### If Corrections Needed

1. Click **"Återställ till Preliminär"** (yellow button)
2. Make your corrections in **"Registrera Resultat"**
3. Click **"Skapa Resultatlista"** again to update
4. Review the changes
5. Click **"Markera som Officiell"** again when satisfied

## For Spectators

### Viewing Results During Competition

1. Navigate to Competition → **Resultat Hub** → **Slutresultat**
2. Page updates automatically every 15 seconds
3. You'll see:
   - Current placement in each shooting class
   - Individual series scores
   - Total score and X count
   - **"PRELIMINÄR"** badge indicating results are still being updated

### Viewing Final Results

1. Same location: Competition → **Resultat Hub** → **Slutresultat**
2. When official:
   - Shows **"OFFICIELL"** badge (green)
   - No more auto-refresh
   - Results are final
3. Use the **"Skriv ut"** button to print results
4. Use the **"Exportera"** button for digital copy

## Status Indicators

| Status | Badge Color | Auto-Refresh | Meaning |
|--------|-------------|--------------|---------|
| **PRELIMINÄR** | Yellow ⚠️ | Every 15 sec | Results still being entered |
| **OFFICIELL** | Green ✅ | Disabled | Final, published results |

## Tips

- **Preliminary results** can be viewed by anyone during the competition
- **Multiple screens**: Display the results page on a TV/monitor for shooters to see
- **Concurrent entry**: Multiple range officers can work simultaneously without conflicts
- **Save often**: Each save immediately updates the database
- **Regenerate list**: Click "Skapa Resultatlista" anytime to update with latest entries
- **Auto-pause**: Auto-refresh pauses automatically when you switch browser tabs

## Common Questions

**Q: Why don't I see my just-entered results?**  
A: Click "Skapa Resultatlista" to regenerate the results list with your new entries.

**Q: Can multiple range officers work at the same time?**  
A: Yes! The system handles concurrent entries. Just make sure each RO works on different shooters.

**Q: How often do results update?**  
A: Preliminary results auto-refresh every 15 seconds. Official results don't auto-refresh.

**Q: Can I undo making results official?**  
A: Yes! Use "Återställ till Preliminär" to revert, make corrections, then mark official again.

**Q: What if someone is already editing a shooter I need to edit?**  
A: The system will warn you. Wait for them to finish, then try again.

## Troubleshooting

**Results not showing:**
- Verify "Skapa Resultatlista" has been clicked
- Check auto-refresh is not paused
- Try manually refreshing the page

**Can't mark as official:**
- Ensure you're logged in as an administrator
- Check that results list has been created first

**Auto-refresh not working:**
- Only works for preliminary results
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure you haven't paused it manually
